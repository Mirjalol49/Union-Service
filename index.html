Engine overhaul

Our comprehensive overhaul service revitalizes high-mileage or neglected engines. Our technicians inspect, clean, and replace worn or damaged components to restore your engine to its original factory specifications or better. Trust us to save you money on costly repairs and get you back on the road.

Body Work

Body work consists of all the steps required to repair a damaged panel up to the paint prep stage. These steps include assessing the damage, grinding, repreparing and reshaping the metal.

Fuel System Cleaning

Clean the gas pump, fuel lines and injectors by adding cleaner to the gas and running the engine. Add a second cleaner directly to the warm engine to remove gunk and carbon deposits from the combustion chambers.
